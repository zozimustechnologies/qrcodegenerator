Get started in 30 seconds

Install the extension

Get it from the Edge Add-ons store.

Open the side panel

Click the QR Code Generator icon in your toolbar. The extension opens in the browser sidebar.

Enter your text or URL

Type or paste any URL, link, or text into the input field.

Generate & scan

Click Generate QR Code — your QR code appears instantly. Scan it with any mobile device.
